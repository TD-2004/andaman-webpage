by air

Flight from Kolkata (Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport, CCU) to Port Blair (Veer Savarkar International Airport, IXZ) Reach CCU by either taxi, private car, or public transportation (such as bus or metro) depending on your location in Kolkata. Check-in and Security Check: Arrive at the airport well in advance to complete check-in procedures and security checks. Arrival at Port Blair (IXZ): Upon arrival at Veer Savarkar International Airport in Port Blair Enjoy Your Stay in Andaman Islands:

by ship

Ships to Andaman usually depart from Kolkata's Haldia Port or Chennai Port. Haldia Port is closer to Kolkata and may have direct sailings to Port Blair.Reach the designated port (Haldia or Chennai) at least a few hours before departure. Plan your transportation to the port in advance, as it might be a distance away from central Kolkata.Ships usually stop at various islands en route, offering passengers a chance to enjoy the scenic beauty of the Bay of Bengal.
